# Wiki Lint

Method:

1. Read `wiki/index.md`, `wiki/log.md`, `wiki/synthesis/map.md`, and `keeper/INSIGHTS.md`.
2. Find missing pages, orphan pages, unresolved contradictions, stale synthesis, weak evidence, and noisy ontology.
3. Convert findings into concrete maintenance actions in `wiki/synthesis/maintenance-plan.md`.
