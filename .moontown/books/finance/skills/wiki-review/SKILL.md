# Wiki Review

Method:

1. Read `wiki/reviews/pending.md`, `wiki/reviews/approved.md`, and `wiki/synthesis/evidence.md`.
2. Check support, dates, confidence, and whether the ontology is clean and stable.
3. Approve, reject, or request revision with explicit reasons.
