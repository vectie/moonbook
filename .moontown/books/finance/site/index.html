Three layers

Keep the system legible at every altitude.

01

Markdown book

Authored pages, navigation, and static rendering for canonical docs.

02

Maintained wiki

Persistent entities, concepts, synthesis, claims, review queues, and keeper memory.

03

Demo website

A marketing-oriented presentation surface for customers, teammates, and operators, copied into `book/site/` during build.

Why this shape

One workspace, three audiences.

Operators

Need the live wiki and review queues to stay cumulative instead of chat-fragmented.

Teammates

Need documentation and structure that still feels deterministic and local-first.

Visitors

Need a crisp story, not the full internals. The marketing projection gives them that surface.
