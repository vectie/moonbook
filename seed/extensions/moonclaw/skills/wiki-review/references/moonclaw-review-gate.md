# MoonClaw Review Gate

## Purpose

This reference is for extension-side review of MoonClaw wiki-maintainer runs.

## Accept only when

- the gather stage inspected real sources
- bootstrap packets exist when needed
- substantive `wiki/*` pages were revised
- the summary matches the artifacts

## Reject or revise when

- the result is generic
- the output is administrative-only
- the ontology became noisier
- the evidence trail is weak

## Operator-facing phrasing

Prefer short explicit findings such as:

- `Rejected: no substantive maintained pages were created.`
- `Revise: packet quality is acceptable, but the concept split is still too noisy.`
