# Query Promotion Contract

## Purpose

Use this reference when deciding whether an answer should remain ephemeral or become a durable wiki asset.

## Promote when

- the answer synthesizes multiple maintained pages
- the answer is likely to be reused
- the answer resolves recurring confusion
- the answer improves the wiki, not just the chat

## Do not promote when

- the answer is only a restatement of one page
- the evidence is still too thin
- the answer depends on unstable speculation
- the question was purely situational

## Preferred targets

- `wiki/queries/*.md` for reusable answer pages
- `wiki/concepts/*.md` for recurring ideas clarified by the answer
- `wiki/entities/*.md` for entity-specific corrections
- `wiki/synthesis/overview.md` for cross-page improvements

## Required saved-query sections

- question
- answer summary
- cited pages
- uncertainty
- follow-ups

## Promotion blocker example

If the answer is useful but under-supported, write a blocker memory candidate instead of a weak durable page.
